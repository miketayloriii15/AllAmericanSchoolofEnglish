Meet Our Instructors

Learn more about the dedicated professionals who will guide you in mastering American English skills. Our instructors are here to support your language journey with expert knowledge and personalized teaching.

Mike Taylor III

Originally from New Orleans, Louisiana, Mike is the founder and lead instructor of the All American School of English. With a TESOL certification and over five years of teaching experience, he specializes in American English, offering a personalized approach to help each student thrive. Mike is also a skilled web developer and professional football player, holding degrees in Computer Science and Information Technology. Alongside English, he speaks some Polish and German—a skill inspired by his Polish fiancée, a professional volleyball player he met in college in the United States, who now manages the school’s social media. With his diverse background and deep commitment, Mike provides an engaging and effective learning experience for students pursuing excellence in English.

Aleksandra Anna Kazala

Aleksandra Kazala, originally from Rzeszów, Poland, is a dedicated language instructor fluent in both English and Polish. She holds degrees in Business Administration and Logistics and has a TESOL certification, with over three years of experience teaching English. As a professional volleyball player who attended college in the United States, Aleksandra combines her international perspective with a passion for education. In addition to teaching English, she offers Polish language lessons, providing learners with an immersive experience in her native language. Currently, Aleksandra is expanding her linguistic skills by learning Spanish. She met her American fiancé, Mike Taylor III, during college, and together, they share a commitment to helping students achieve their language goals.
